"Nanak Naam Chardi Kala, Tere Bhane Sarbat Da Bhala"

Waheguru Ji se yahi prarthana hai ki aap hamesha tandurust raho aur muskurati raho.

Waheguru ji,
Mere dost ko hamesha sehat, sukoon aur himmat dena. Uske dil ki tension halka karna aur uske raaste asaan karna.

Wherever life feels heavy, please wrap my friend in Your peace and protection. May calm replace worry, and hope replace fear.
